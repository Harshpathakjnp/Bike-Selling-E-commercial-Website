1.Revolt RV 400

Start @ Rs 1.29 lakh

Battery Range 150km/Full charge
Weight - 108 kg

The Revolt RV400 is the first AI-enabled electric motorcycle in the Indian market. The Revolt RV 400 electric
motorcycle is offered in two variants: Standard and premium.
It has a muscular design and comes with lots of features like side stand sensor, combi brake system, motorcycle sounds, etc. The Revolt RV400 is powered by a
3.24kWh battery pack that sends power to a 3,000 Watt motor. It has a claimed range of around 85 kilometres in Sport mode, 100 kilometres in Normal mode and 150
kilometres in Eco mode. It takes around 4.5 hours to charge up the battery.

Etryst-350 Model

Start @ Rs 1.54 lakh

Battery Range 140km/Full charge
Weight - 120 kg

PURE EV Etryst-350 is an electric street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,54,999 in India. It is available in only 1

BGauss A2

Start @ Rs 0.52 lakh

Battery Range 70km/Full charge
Weight - 108 kg

BGauss A2 is an electric scooter available at a starting price of Rs. 52,499 in India. It is available in 2 variants and 3 colours
with top variant price starting from Rs. 68,496. BGauss A2 generates 250 W power from its motor. BGauss A2 comes up with both front and rear disc brakes.
Bgauss A2 is a part of the two-product portfolio from the Indian electric scooter brand. The A2 features a modern design that comprises of apron-mounted
